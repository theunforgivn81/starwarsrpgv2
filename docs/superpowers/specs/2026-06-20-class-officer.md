## 1. Identity

*A leader who turns a squad into a unit.* **Key attribute: Charisma. HP 8.** The Officer **Commands** — issuing **Directives** that make allies' own actions land harder, and leading by example by wading in itself. It's the galaxy's premier **face** and the "force-multiplier" support engine, with the deepest skill bench alongside the Tech Specialist.

*"You play a leader who turns a squad into a unit, and on a typical turn you issue a Directive — focus fire, reposition, rally — that buffs your allies' actions, then lead by example by Striking or moving in yourself."*

**Uniqueness (the verb):** **Command** — Directives that **force-multiply** the party. Its claim is making *other* characters better, the only class whose power is mostly expressed on *allies'* turns.

**Role profile (chassis):** Support 3 · Social 2 · Utility 2 · Control 1 · Single-target 1 · Durability 1 · Area 0 *(sum 10).* The chassis is the **buff/leader engine + face**; the Leadership Style steers it (Field Commander→frontline grants, Spymaster→debuff/intel, Inspiring Hero→morale/rally).

## 4. The Verb — Command (the Directive engine)

A typical turn: issue a **Directive** (focus-fire, reposition, rally) that makes your allies' *own* actions better this round, and **lead by example** by Striking or moving in yourself. The decisions — *which* Directive, *which* target/allies, whether to spend the extra action to lead — are the variety.

### 4.1 Directives — and the action-granting rule *(the resolved Gate-A risk)*

- **Directives** are orders that **benefit allies who follow them**; allies must be able to **sense you** (auditory or visual). You **issue one Directive per round**, lasting until the start of your next turn. You start knowing one (**Get-the-Job-Done**, below) and learn more via the class, feats, and your Leadership Style.
- **Bonus-first — the action-economy safeguard.** By default, a Directive grants a **status bonus** to allies' actions (to attacks, damage, AC, saves, or Speed) — it makes the team's *own* turns count for more. **It does *not* grant a free action.** This is *why* the Officer force-multiplies without being **action-positive** (the [Gate-A](2026-06-19-gate-a-balance-pass.md) red-flag root): bonuses scale with allies but never net the party extra actions.
- **Action-granting is the bounded exception.** A few Directives *do* grant an ally a **limited** action — a **Step** or a **single Strike** — and these are priced at **≈ 1:1** (the Officer spends ~the action it grants) and capped by the **one-Directive-per-round** limit. **Never "a full extra turn / 2+ actions."** *(This is the line Gate-A validates: a repeatable >1:1 action-grant is the degenerate root; we forbid it by rule.)*
- **Lead by Example.** Spending the **2nd action** on a Directive lets the Officer **also act** (Strike/move) and deepens the Directive — so the Officer participates rather than standing back as a pure buff-bot (keeping the *Officer's* turn engaging, and the buff honest).

**Get-the-Job-Done** `[1 action]` or `[2 actions]` *(Directive; the starting one, adapted from Get 'Em)*: choose an enemy within 60 ft you can see; you and allies who can sense you gain a **+1 status bonus to attacks** against it this round. **Lead by Example** (2 actions): also **Strike** it (+CHA status to your damage), and you and your allies gain **+1 status to damage** against it this round.

### 4.2 Flourish

Splashy command techniques carry the **`[Flourish]`** trait — **one per turn** (a dramatic rally, a cutting taunt, a battlefield gambit), keeping turns varied.

### 4.3 ✦ development

- **L3 Adaptive Talent** — a flexible pick (a skill feat / a general feat you can swap), the adaptable-leader feature.
- **L5 / scaling — Directives known** grow; Get-the-Job-Done's bonus damage scales (5/11/17).
- **L13 Tactician** — issue a Directive as part of another action once/round, or maintain a second Directive (lets you command *and* act more fluidly).
- **L15 Legendary Improvisation** — attempt skills untrained as if trained (the do-anything leader).
- **L20 Unify the Squad** *(capstone)* — your Directive can affect the whole party at once / a powerful army-of-one command. *(Exact form Gate-A.)*

## 5. Subclasses — Leadership Styles *(to design next — ~3, SF2E Envoy-adapted)*

Branch at **L1** on the **1/3/7/11/15/19** cadence; each grants a leadership skill + perk + a signature Directive, steering Command. Sketched ([roster §6](2026-06-15-subclass-roster-sketch.md)) + the prior Envoy styles (From the Front / From the Shadows / In the Spotlight):
- **Field Commander** (From the Front) — frontline leader: Directives that grant **movement/positioning** and let you fight alongside (the bounded action-grant fork).
- **Spymaster** (From the Shadows) — intel/deception: debuff foes, coordinate ambushes, information (Control/Social).
- **Inspiring Hero** (In the Spotlight) — morale/rally: buffs, rally-from-the-brink, temp HP, the social spotlight (Support).
