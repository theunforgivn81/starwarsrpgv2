## 1. Identity

*A Force mystic who reshapes the battlefield with will alone.* **Key attribute: Charisma. HP 8.** The Consular is **the caster** — it **Manifests** Force powers from the deepest Attunement pool and the broadest school access of any class, bending foes and terrain to its will (telekinesis, mind-domination, zones, healing). It carries a lightsaber only as a last resort (and to Deflect).

*"You play a Force mystic who manifests powers, and on a typical turn you hurl a telekinetic impact, then spend Attunement to crush, dominate, mend, or wall off the battlefield — your will is the weapon."*

**Key-attribute divergence (CHA, not ORC's WIS):** locked in the [roster](2026-06-15-class-roster-sketch.md) (full-spread rework, like Soldier DEX→CON and Guardian WIS→STR). The **Sentinel** owns WIS (Perception→Legendary, the perceiving reactor); the Consular takes **CHA** so its Manifest reads as *force of will* and it can anchor the Force **social/domination** engine. Its Force DC and Manifest powers key off **CHA**. *(One-line swap to WIS if ever reversed.)*

**Uniqueness (the verb):** **Manifest** — deepest Attunement, broadest toolkit access, **Legendary Class DC** (its save-DC *is* its weapon — the offensive spotlight, mirroring the Guardian's Legendary saber), plus an **at-will** Force attack so it never gases out.

**Role profile (chassis):** Control 3 · Area 2 · Utility 2 · Single-target 1 · Support 1 · Social 1 · Durability 1 *(sum 11).* The chassis is the **controller-caster**; the **Mandate** (subclass) steers it — Vitality→Support, Dominance→Social, Telekinesis→Area/Control, etc. (breadth lives across Mandates, so no single build is a spotlight monopoly).

## 4. The Verb — Manifest

A typical Consular turn: an **at-will Telekinetic Impact** (reliable offense, no resource), then **spend Attunement** on a Force power — crush, hold, dominate, mend, or wall off — and **Center** to refuel when the pool runs low. The decisions are *which power, which target, how much to Push, when to refuel* — pure caster resource-management, not "Strike ×3."

### 4.1 Manifest — the framework

- **Deepest pool + best refuel (Deep Reservoir):** the Consular's Attunement pool is **`3 + L`** (one deeper than other Force users' `2 + L`) and **Center restores +3** (vs +2). It is the Force-resource class. *(Values Gate-A.)*
- **Broadest access:** the Consular can learn and Manifest powers from **all six [schools](2026-06-15-shared-force-toolkit.md)** (Guardian/Sentinel lean 1–3); it knows the **most powers** of any class (a "powers known" that grows across levels) and its **Mandate** deepens one school.
- **CHA Class DC:** Manifested save-DC powers use the Consular's **Class DC** (→ Legendary). *(No attribute restate — the Class DC already includes CHA.)*
- **Push** (the toolkit's +1-Att upgrade) and the **Greater-tier gates** ([toolkit §5](2026-06-15-shared-force-toolkit.md)) apply as written — the Consular is simply the class that reaches and fuels them best.

### 4.2 Telekinetic Impact `[1 action]` or `[2 actions]` — the at-will attack

A signature **at-will** ranged Force attack (**0 Attunement**), so the caster always has reliable offense without draining its pool. 1 action = a quick kinetic jolt; 2 actions = a heavier impact (more dice). Damage scales on the standard auto-scaling dice track ([toolkit §3.1](2026-06-15-shared-force-toolkit.md)); it uses the Consular's class statistic to hit. *(Replaces ORC's Connected-gated version — ours is simply at-will, since our Attunement economy already does the "channeling" job; no Connected state needed.)*

### 4.3 ✦ development

- **L5 Heightened Manifest** — you learn more powers and may **Push** one power per turn for free (no extra Attunement); a second power's worth of throughput.
- **L9 Greater Manifest** — your access to **Greater** (Tier III) powers opens; pool/known grow.
- **L13 Master Manifest** — Tier IV power access; your Telekinetic Impact gains an additional action mode / rider.
- **L17 Force Mastery** — Class DC → Legendary; your save-DC powers are hardest in the galaxy to resist.
- **L20 Avatar of the Force** *(capstone)* — the apex caster (e.g., once per round, Manifest a power **without spending Attunement**, or treat your pool as full once per encounter). *(Exact form Gate-A.)*

### 4.4 Lightsaber & Deflect

The Consular carries a lightsaber for last resort and **Deflect** (the shared toolkit power — it learns it like any Force user). It can dabble in **Forms** (especially **Niman**, the blade↔Force balance Form) via the [Forms portability](2026-06-18-lightsaber-forms.md) — base stance only (no Guardian Mastery).

## 5. Subclasses — Mandates (six, one per Force school) *(to design next)*

Branch at **L1** on the **1/3/7/11/15/19** cadence; each **Mandate** = a Force **school** specialization (signature school free + bonus powers + synergy). Per [subclass-counts], the Consular gets **one per school** (6):

| Mandate | School | Emphasis |
|---|---|---|
| **Telekinesis** | Alter | kinetic control — throw/crush/hold/zones |
| **Visions** | Sense | foresight, detection, initiative/defense buffs |
| **Serenity** | Control | energy defense, barriers, Tutaminis, cloak |
| **Vitality** | Body | the Force healer — the optional party heal at its strongest |
| **Dominance** | Mind | the mentalist — fear/influence/domination; the social engine |
| **Destruction** | Offense | raw Force offense — lightning, blasts (`[dark]`-adjacent) |

*(Each modulates Manifest toward its school; balanced cross-Mandate. `[dark]` powers in Destruction/Dominance/Vitality hook the deferred corruption subsystem.)*
