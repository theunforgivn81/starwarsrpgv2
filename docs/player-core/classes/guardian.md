# Guardian

*The squad broke and ran, and he did not. He set his stance in the breach, and every bolt that should have found a fleeing back found his blade instead — and the ones his blade couldn't reach, he simply refused to let land.*

You stand between danger and the people you've sworn to protect. You're a Force warrior shaped by discipline — the endless drilling of the lightsaber forms — and you wear no heavy plate, because your blade is your armor and theirs. Your companions take risks they never otherwise could, knowing that when it goes wrong you'll already be there; you've built your whole life around being there. You would rather carry a wound than watch a friend take one. You might be a knight sworn to an ancient order, a temple guardian, a bodyguard bound by a debt of honor, or the keeper of an oath only you remember.

> **Key Attribute** Strength
> **Hit Points** 10 + your Constitution modifier (each level)

A high Strength drives your lightsaber, your telekinetic shoves, and the force of your interceding blows. Strength is your attack key and the basis of much of what you do; Constitution keeps you standing in the fire you draw.

> **Sidebar — Reading an Ability Block**
> Activated abilities are written as blocks. The symbol after the name is the action cost: **[one-action]**, **[two-actions]**, **[three-actions]**, **[reaction]**, or **[free-action]**. *Traits* note the rules categories an ability belongs to. *MAP* is your multiple attack penalty; reaction Strikes ignore it.

> **Sidebar — Forms and Powers Live in *The Force***
> The Guardian wields the Force's blade. Your **Lightsaber Forms** and the powers you channel (telekinetic shoves, **Deflect**, and more) all live in the **The Force** chapter, along with **Attunement** — the resource that fuels them. This chapter covers what makes you their master.

---

## Key Terms

**Guardian Mastery**. The upgrade clause every Lightsaber Form carries for those who have truly mastered the Forms. As a Guardian, you gain it on every Form you enter — others get only the base stance.

**Blade-Flow**. Your signature command of the Forms: you enter and switch stances as a free action, flowing into your guard in the same breath you fight, and you benefit from every Form's Guardian Mastery.

**Intercede**. Your signature reaction: throwing yourself — body or will — into a blow meant for an ally.

**Form**. A lightsaber fighting stance (see *The Force*). Your subclass is built around one Form you master from 1st level.

*(For Attunement, Deflect, and the full Form rules, see **The Force**.)*

## Roleplaying the Guardian

### During Combat Encounters…

You move to the front and meet the most dangerous foe on the field blade to blade, turning aside the fire and blows meant for your allies. As the fight shifts, you flow from one lightsaber form to the next — a wall one moment, a hammer the next.

### During Social Encounters…

You're the steadfast one — the bodyguard, the protector, the line nobody crosses. When you give your word, others know you mean to keep it, whatever it costs you.

### While Exploring…

You take point into danger and check the dark corners first. When the others make it out, it's usually because you stood where they couldn't.

### In Downtime…

You drill your forms relentlessly, tend your blade, and stand watch over those in your care.

### You Might…

- Have sworn yourself to a person, a place, or an ideal, and hold that vow above your own life.
- Carry the discipline of a warrior tradition, and find your calm inside it.
- Believe the truest measure of your strength is the harm you keep others from suffering.

### Others Probably…

- Fight more boldly, knowing you'll catch the blow that gets through.
- Put you between themselves and the worst thing on the field.
- Count on you to be the last one standing.

## Blade-Flow

**(1st level)** You are a master of the **Lightsaber Forms** (see *The Force*), and you move through them like water. Whenever you're in a stance granted by a Lightsaber Form, you gain the benefit of its **Guardian Mastery** clause, not just its base effect. You begin play knowing your subclass's signature Form for free, and you can learn other Forms with your class feats.

Where others must spend an action to set or change their stance, you flow into your guard in the same breath you draw your blade:

**Blade-Flow**  [free-action]  · **Frequency** once per round · **Traits** Guardian
**Trigger** You roll initiative, or it's your turn.
**Effect** You enter a Form you know, or switch from the Form you're in to another — the flow between stances that gives the discipline its name.

## Intercede

**Intercede**  [reaction]  · **Traits** Guardian
**Trigger** An enemy damages an ally within 15 feet of you that you can perceive.
**Effect** You throw yourself — or your will — into the blow. Reduce the damage your ally takes by **2 + your level**, and until the end of your next turn you gain a **+2 circumstance bonus to attack rolls against that enemy**.

## Guardian's Reach

**Guardian's Reach**  [reaction]  · **Traits** Guardian
**Trigger** A creature within your reach uses a move action to leave a square within your reach.
**Effect** You make a melee lightsaber Strike against it at full accuracy (this Strike ignores your multiple attack penalty). The creature's movement still happens — the Strike is the price of leaving your guard. A foe can **Tumble Through** your space (Acrobatics against your Guardian class DC) to move without provoking this. Guardian's Reach shares your reaction budget with Deflect and Intercede.

## Deflect

**(1st level)** You learn the **Deflect** power (see *The Force*). Your circumstance bonus to AC from Deflect is **+3** rather than +2, and some Forms extend it further.

## Form

**(1st level)** Every Guardian masters a signature **Form** — your subclass. It grants that Form's stance for free (with its Guardian Mastery upgrade), a synergy that sharpens your Intercede, and deeper features at 3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th, and 19th levels.

## Guardian Weapon Mastery

**(5th level)** Your proficiency ranks for unarmed attacks, simple weapons, and martial weapons increase to expert. Your proficiency rank with lightsabers increases to master.

## Armor Expertise

**(7th level)** Your proficiency ranks for light armor, medium armor, and unarmored defense increase to expert.

## Vigilant Guard

**(7th level)** Your active defense comes fully online. You gain a **second reaction each round**, which you can use only for Deflect, Intercede, or Guardian's Reach. (You still can't exceed three reactions in a round.)

## Weapon Specialization

**(7th level)** You inflict greater injuries with the weapons you know best. You deal 2 additional damage with weapons and unarmed attacks in which you're an expert. This damage increases to 3 if you're a master, and 4 if you're legendary.

At **15th level**, this becomes **Greater Weapon Specialization**: 4 additional damage as an expert, 6 as a master, and 8 as a legendary.

## Resolve

**(7th level)** Your discipline is unshakable. Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to master, and when you roll a success on a Will save, you get a critical success instead.

## Guardian Expertise

**(9th level)** Your proficiency rank for your Guardian class DC increases to expert.

## Improved Intercede

**(9th level)** Your protection deepens. Intercede now reduces the damage your ally takes by **4 + your level + your Strength modifier**.

## Reflex Expertise

**(9th level)** Your proficiency rank for Reflex saves increases to expert.

## Perception Expertise

**(9th level)** Your proficiency rank for Perception increases to expert.

## Juggernaut

**(11th level)** You shrug off what would fell others. Your proficiency rank for Fortitude saves increases to master, and when you roll a success on a Fortitude save, you get a critical success instead.

## Greater Blade-Flow

**(13th level)** Your flow is seamless. You can use **Blade-Flow** twice per round, and whenever you switch Forms with it, you keep the stance benefit of the Form you left until the start of your next turn — for that span you fight in two Forms at once.

## Armor Mastery

**(13th level)** Your proficiency ranks for light armor, medium armor, and unarmored defense increase to master.

## Lightsaber Legend

**(13th level)** Your proficiency rank for lightsabers increases to legendary — the premier blade in the galaxy. Your proficiency ranks for unarmed attacks, simple weapons, and martial weapons increase to master.

## Greater Resolve

**(15th level)** Your proficiency rank for Will saves increases to legendary. When you roll a critical failure on a Will save, you get a failure instead, and when you fail a Will save against an effect that deals damage, you take half that damage.

## Greater Guardian Expertise

**(17th level)** Your proficiency rank for your Guardian class DC increases to master.

## Legendary Armor

**(17th level)** Your proficiency ranks for light armor, medium armor, and unarmored defense increase to legendary.

## Master's Guard

**(17th level)** You are the perfect bodyguard. When you Intercede, the triggering damage can't reduce your ally below 1 Hit Point.

## Guardian Forms

Choose one of the seven Forms below when you make your Guardian (see the *Form* class feature). The base stances and the Form feats you can branch into are in **[The Force](the-force.md)**; this is the deep mastery your subclass adds on top. Each Form's **3rd-level** feature reshapes how your signature Form uses Intercede, your bodyguard's reaction.

> **Sidebar — You Still Flow**
> Your subclass is the Form you *master*, not the only one you can use. With your class feats you learn other Forms and flow between them freely in combat — your Guardian Mastery applies to every Form you enter, not just your signature one.

### Soresu — Way of the Mynock

*The immovable wall.* Soresu is pure defense — the Form that turns a storm of blasterfire into so much noise.

#### Resolute Guard
**(1st level)** You gain the **Soresu** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While you're in Soresu, your Speed penalty is only **−5 feet** (instead of −10), and your **Deflect** bonus improves to **+4**.

#### Sphere of Protection
**(3rd level)** While you're in Soresu, allies within 10 feet share your **+2 circumstance bonus to AC against ranged Strikes** — the eye of the storm spreads to those behind you.

#### Deflecting Wall
**(7th level)** Your **Deflect** can protect an adjacent ally from a ranged attack, and while you're in Soresu you can Deflect melee attacks as well.

#### Unyielding
**(11th level)** While in Soresu, you gain resistance to physical and energy damage equal to your Strength modifier, and forced movement against you is halved.

#### Eye of the Storm
**(15th level)** Allies within 15 feet of you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC, and your **Intercede** reduces a *ranged* attack's damage to **0**.

#### Immovable Bulwark
**(19th level)** While you're in Soresu, you and allies within 15 feet can't be critically hit by ranged attacks, and once per round you can use Deflect to protect any ally within 15 feet.

---

### Ataru — Way of the Hawk-Bat

*Aggressive grace.* Ataru is leaping, spinning offense — you're never where the enemy expects, and you reach the ally who needs you.

#### Acrobatic Assault
**(1st level)** You gain the **Ataru** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. When you Stride and then make a Strike, your first Strike gains a +1 circumstance bonus to the attack roll.

#### Swift Intercede
**(3rd level)** Your Intercede range increases to **30 feet**, and if the ally is more than 15 feet away, you can Stride to within 15 feet of them as part of the reaction.

#### Hawk-Bat Swoop
**(7th level)** You gain the **Hawk-Bat Swoop** feat (see *The Force*), and your Guardian Mastery lets it make its second Strike.

#### Whirlwind
**(11th level)** After you Tumble Through a creature's space, you can make a lightsaber Strike against it as part of that movement (once per turn), and you gain an additional +10-foot status bonus to Speed.

#### Unstoppable Momentum
**(15th level)** Your movement never triggers reactions, and the off-guard your Tumble Through imposes applies to your allies' attacks as well as your own.

#### Living Tempest
**(19th level)** Once per round you gain an extra action you can use only to Stride or Strike, and when you Intercede you can immediately make a Strike against the triggering enemy.

---

### Shien / Djem So — Way of the Krayt Dragon

*Every blow returned.* This is the counterattacking Form: the harder they hit you, the harder you answer.

#### Crusader's Focus
**(1st level)** You gain the **Shien / Djem So** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While you're in it, if you've taken damage since the end of your last turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your lightsaber damage.

#### Crusader's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, the attacking enemy takes damage equal to your Strength modifier — it pays for the blow it landed on your friend.

#### Riposte
**(7th level)** You gain the **Riposte** reaction (once per round): when an enemy's melee attack misses you, or when you Deflect a ranged attack, make a lightsaber Strike against that enemy. (This uses a reaction, within your three-per-round limit.)

#### Shien Deflection
**(11th level)** When you Deflect a ranged attack, you can redirect it — make a lightsaber Strike against the attacker or another enemy within range.

#### Vengeful Intercession
**(15th level)** After you Intercede, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to damage against that enemy for 1 round, and your Riposte against it deals an additional weapon damage die.

#### Unbreakable Counter
**(19th level)** Whenever an enemy hits you or an ally you've Interceded for, you can Strike it (once per round, separate from Riposte), and your counterattacks deal additional damage equal to your Strength modifier.

---

### Makashi — Way of the Ysalamiri

*The duelist's art.* Makashi locks down a single foe and dismantles it with precision.

#### Duelist's Flourish
**(1st level)** You gain the **Makashi** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. Against your **Dueling Target**, your lightsaber Strikes gain a +1 circumstance bonus to the attack roll.

#### Duelist's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you can Step to place yourself between the attacker and the ally (moving toward either).

#### Contentious Riposte
**(7th level)** You gain the **Contentious Riposte** feat (see *The Force*), and you can choose to Disarm your Dueling Target with it instead of Striking.

#### Sun Djem
**(11th level)** You can attempt to Disarm your Dueling Target as part of a lightsaber Strike, and a target you disarm is off-guard to you until the end of your next turn.

#### Bind the Blade
**(15th level)** Your Dueling Target takes a **−2 circumstance penalty to attack anyone but you** — the duelist's soft taunt, drawing the deadliest foe onto your blade.

#### Master Duelist
**(19th level)** Your Dueling Target is permanently off-guard to you, you score a critical hit against it on a 19–20, and when it attacks anyone but you, you can Strike it (once per round, free).

---

### Niman — Way of the Rancor

*Balance of blade and Force.* Niman flows between the lightsaber and the power, each feeding the other.

#### Flowing Force
**(1st level)** You gain the **Niman** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. Once per turn while you're in Niman, a telekinetic shove (such as Force Push or Telekinetic Grasp) costs 1 less Attunement (minimum 0).

#### Balanced Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you can spend 1 Attunement to also push the attacker 5 feet — a telekinetic cushion that becomes a shove.

#### Saber Throw
**(7th level)** You add the **Saber Throw** power to your repertoire (see *The Force*), and while you're in Niman its Strikes carry your Niman synergy bonus.

#### Force-Blade Fusion
**(11th level)** Once per turn when you make a lightsaber Strike, you can add a telekinetic Shove or Reposition (using your Guardian class DC) as a free action, and your Niman synergy bonus rises to +2.

#### Telekinetic Guard
**(15th level)** You can Intercede for an ally within 30 feet even without a clear line to them — you feel the blow coming — and you reduce the damage by an additional amount equal to your Strength modifier.

#### Unity of Blade and Force
**(19th level)** Once per round, you can Manifest a Force power and make a lightsaber Strike as a single two-action activity, and your Niman synergy bonuses are always active while you're in the stance.

---

### Shii-Cho — Way of the Sarlacc

*Hold the line against many.* Shii-Cho is the Form of the outnumbered — the one who stands amid a crowd and keeps them all at bay.

#### Sweeping Guard
**(1st level)** You gain the **Shii-Cho** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While two or more enemies are adjacent to you, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC — you're in your element amid a crowd.

#### Vanguard's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you push the attacking enemy 5 feet away from the ally after the attack resolves.

#### Cyclone
**(7th level)** You gain the **Cyclone** feat (see *The Force*) — one lightsaber Strike against each enemy in your reach.

#### Hold the Line
**(11th level)** Enemies adjacent to you take a **−1 circumstance penalty to attack anyone but you** — the crowd-wide cousin of the duelist's lock, drawing a whole mob's attention onto you.

#### Sweeping Bulwark
**(15th level)** Once per round, when an adjacent enemy attacks one of your allies, you can Strike it as a reaction. Enemies that begin their turn adjacent to you are off-guard to you.

#### Unbroken Line
**(19th level)** You can Intercede for any ally adjacent to an enemy within your reach, and once per round, while two or more enemies are adjacent to you, you can use Cyclone as a free action.

---

### Juyo / Vaapad — Way of the Vornskr

*Controlled fury.* The forbidden seventh Form trades safety for overwhelming threat — you tank by being too dangerous to ignore. Its techniques carry the **Dark** trait.

#### Vaapad's Fury
**(1st level)** You gain the **Juyo / Vaapad** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery (a +2 circumstance bonus to attack rolls). While you're in it, after you take *or* deal damage, your next lightsaber Strike this round gains a +1 circumstance bonus to damage.

#### Furious Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to attack the triggering enemy, and your next Strike against it deals additional damage equal to your Strength modifier — you turn the fury of its blow back on it.

#### Relentless Onslaught
**(7th level)** Once per round, after a lightsaber Strike hits, you can make an additional Strike at your current multiple attack penalty. You're then off-guard until the start of your next turn — the price of total commitment.

#### Feed the Fury
**(11th level)** When an enemy hits you, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack and damage against it until the start of your next turn, and while in Juyo your attack bonus rises to +3 (your AC penalty stays −1).

#### Unrelenting
**(15th level)** You ignore the off-guard condition caused by your own Juyo techniques, and your lightsaber critical hits make the target frightened 1.

#### Avatar of Fury
**(19th level)** Once per round you gain an extra action you can use only for a lightsaber Strike, and while you're below half your Hit Points you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to attack and damage rolls.

## Guardian Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Guardian class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower.

> **Sidebar — Lightsaber Form Feats Are Guardian Feats**
> Your deepest pool of options isn't on this page — it's the **Lightsaber Form feats** in **[The Force](the-force.md)**. You can spend any Guardian class feat to learn a new Form or a Form chain feat (Cyclone, Hawk-Bat Swoop, Contentious Riposte, and the rest), and as a Guardian you gain every Form's Guardian Mastery upgrade. Branching into new Forms and deepening the ones you know is the core of building a Guardian; the feats below are everything *else* — the telekinetics, the guarding tools, and the all-purpose tricks that work no matter which stance you're in.

---

### 1st-Level Feats

#### Challenging Shout  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian, Auditory
An enemy you can see takes a **−1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls that don't include you** until the start of your next turn — the bodyguard's dare.

#### Steel Mind
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against fear and mental effects, and once per round you can reduce the value of one mental condition affecting you by 1 as a free action.

#### Telekinetic Leap  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian
**Cost** 1 Attunement
**Effect** You Leap with a burst of the Force, clearing a long gap or closing on a distant foe — the melee guardian's way to reach the firing line.

> No matter how many reactions your features and feats grant, you can use **at most three per round**. Vigilant Guard, Combat Reflexes, and Greater Combat Reflexes raise you *toward* that ceiling, never past it — so a Guardian chooses which triggers are worth answering.

#### Shielding Intercede
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Guardian
