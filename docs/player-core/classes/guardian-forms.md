# Guardian Forms

At 1st level, every Guardian masters a signature **Form** — the lightsaber discipline that defines how you fight and guard. Your Form grants its stance for free (with the **Guardian Mastery** upgrade), a synergy that sharpens your Blade-Flow and Intercede, and deeper features at **3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th, and 19th** levels.

Choose one of the seven below when you make your Guardian. The base stances and the Form feats you can branch into are in **[The Force](the-force.md)**; this is the deep mastery your subclass adds on top. Each Form's **3rd-level** feature reshapes how your signature Form uses Intercede, your bodyguard's reaction.

> **Sidebar — You Still Flow**
> Your subclass is the Form you *master*, not the only one you can use. With your class feats you learn other Forms and flow between them freely in combat — your Guardian Mastery applies to every Form you enter, not just your signature one.

# Soresu — Way of the Mynock

*The immovable wall.* Soresu is pure defense — the Form that turns a storm of blasterfire into so much noise.

### Resolute Guard
**(1st level)** You gain the **Soresu** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While you're in Soresu, your Speed penalty is only **−5 feet** (instead of −10), and your **Deflect** bonus improves to **+4**.

### Sphere of Protection
**(3rd level)** While you're in Soresu, allies within 10 feet share your **+2 circumstance bonus to AC against ranged Strikes** — the eye of the storm spreads to those behind you.

### Deflecting Wall
**(7th level)** Your **Deflect** can protect an adjacent ally from a ranged attack, and while you're in Soresu you can Deflect melee attacks as well.

### Unyielding
**(11th level)** While in Soresu, you gain resistance to physical and energy damage equal to your Strength modifier, and forced movement against you is halved.

### Eye of the Storm
**(15th level)** Allies within 15 feet of you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC, and your **Intercede** reduces a *ranged* attack's damage to **0**.

### Immovable Bulwark
**(19th level)** While you're in Soresu, you and allies within 15 feet can't be critically hit by ranged attacks, and once per round you can use Deflect to protect any ally within 15 feet.

# Ataru — Way of the Hawk-Bat

*Aggressive grace.* Ataru is leaping, spinning offense — you're never where the enemy expects, and you reach the ally who needs you.

### Acrobatic Assault
**(1st level)** You gain the **Ataru** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. When you Stride and then make a Strike, your first Strike gains a +1 circumstance bonus to the attack roll.

### Swift Intercede
**(3rd level)** Your Intercede range increases to **30 feet**, and if the ally is more than 15 feet away, you can Stride to within 15 feet of them as part of the reaction.

### Hawk-Bat Swoop
**(7th level)** You gain the **Hawk-Bat Swoop** feat (see *The Force*), and your Guardian Mastery lets it make its second Strike.

### Whirlwind
**(11th level)** After you Tumble Through a creature's space, you can make a lightsaber Strike against it as part of that movement (once per turn), and you gain an additional +10-foot status bonus to Speed.

### Unstoppable Momentum
**(15th level)** Your movement never triggers reactions, and the off-guard your Tumble Through imposes applies to your allies' attacks as well as your own.

### Living Tempest
**(19th level)** Once per round you gain an extra action you can use only to Stride or Strike, and when you Intercede you can immediately make a Strike against the triggering enemy.

# Shien / Djem So — Way of the Krayt Dragon

*Every blow returned.* This is the counterattacking Form: the harder they hit you, the harder you answer.

### Crusader's Focus
**(1st level)** You gain the **Shien / Djem So** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While you're in it, if you've taken damage since the end of your last turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your lightsaber damage.

### Crusader's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, the attacking enemy takes damage equal to your Strength modifier — it pays for the blow it landed on your friend.

### Riposte
**(7th level)** You gain the **Riposte** reaction (once per round): when an enemy's melee attack misses you, or when you Deflect a ranged attack, make a lightsaber Strike against that enemy. (This uses a reaction, within your three-per-round limit.)

### Shien Deflection
**(11th level)** When you Deflect a ranged attack, you can redirect it — make a lightsaber Strike against the attacker or another enemy within range.

### Vengeful Intercession
**(15th level)** After you Intercede, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to damage against that enemy for 1 round, and your Riposte against it deals an additional weapon damage die.

### Unbreakable Counter
**(19th level)** Whenever an enemy hits you or an ally you've Interceded for, you can Strike it (once per round, separate from Riposte), and your counterattacks deal additional damage equal to your Strength modifier.

# Makashi — Way of the Ysalamiri

*The duelist's art.* Makashi locks down a single foe and dismantles it with precision.

### Duelist's Flourish
**(1st level)** You gain the **Makashi** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. Against your **Dueling Target**, your lightsaber Strikes gain a +1 circumstance bonus to the attack roll.

### Duelist's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you can Step to place yourself between the attacker and the ally (moving toward either).

### Contentious Riposte
**(7th level)** You gain the **Contentious Riposte** feat (see *The Force*), and you can choose to Disarm your Dueling Target with it instead of Striking.

### Sun Djem
**(11th level)** You can attempt to Disarm your Dueling Target as part of a lightsaber Strike, and a target you disarm is off-guard to you until the end of your next turn.

### Bind the Blade
**(15th level)** Your Dueling Target takes a **−2 circumstance penalty to attack anyone but you** — the duelist's soft taunt, drawing the deadliest foe onto your blade.

### Master Duelist
**(19th level)** Your Dueling Target is permanently off-guard to you, you score a critical hit against it on a 19–20, and when it attacks anyone but you, you can Strike it (once per round, free).

# Niman — Way of the Rancor

*Balance of blade and Force.* Niman flows between the lightsaber and the power, each feeding the other.

### Flowing Force
**(1st level)** You gain the **Niman** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. Once per turn while you're in Niman, a telekinetic shove (such as Force Push or Telekinetic Grasp) costs 1 less Attunement (minimum 0).

### Balanced Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you can spend 1 Attunement to also push the attacker 5 feet — a telekinetic cushion that becomes a shove.

### Saber Throw
**(7th level)** You add the **Saber Throw** power to your repertoire (see *The Force*), and while you're in Niman its Strikes carry your Niman synergy bonus.

### Force-Blade Fusion
**(11th level)** Once per turn when you make a lightsaber Strike, you can add a telekinetic Shove or Reposition (using your Guardian class DC) as a free action, and your Niman synergy bonus rises to +2.

### Telekinetic Guard
**(15th level)** You can Intercede for an ally within 30 feet even without a clear line to them — you feel the blow coming — and you reduce the damage by an additional amount equal to your Strength modifier.

### Unity of Blade and Force
**(19th level)** Once per round, you can Manifest a Force power and make a lightsaber Strike as a single two-action activity, and your Niman synergy bonuses are always active while you're in the stance.

# Shii-Cho — Way of the Sarlacc

*Hold the line against many.* Shii-Cho is the Form of the outnumbered — the one who stands amid a crowd and keeps them all at bay.

### Sweeping Guard
**(1st level)** You gain the **Shii-Cho** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery. While two or more enemies are adjacent to you, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC — you're in your element amid a crowd.

### Vanguard's Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you push the attacking enemy 5 feet away from the ally after the attack resolves.

### Cyclone
**(7th level)** You gain the **Cyclone** feat (see *The Force*) — one lightsaber Strike against each enemy in your reach.

### Hold the Line
**(11th level)** Enemies adjacent to you take a **−1 circumstance penalty to attack anyone but you** — the crowd-wide cousin of the duelist's lock, drawing a whole mob's attention onto you.

### Sweeping Bulwark
**(15th level)** Once per round, when an adjacent enemy attacks one of your allies, you can Strike it as a reaction. Enemies that begin their turn adjacent to you are off-guard to you.

### Unbroken Line
**(19th level)** You can Intercede for any ally adjacent to an enemy within your reach, and once per round, while two or more enemies are adjacent to you, you can use Cyclone as a free action.

# Juyo / Vaapad — Way of the Vornskr

*Controlled fury.* The forbidden seventh Form trades safety for overwhelming threat — you tank by being too dangerous to ignore. Its techniques carry the **Dark** trait.

### Vaapad's Fury
**(1st level)** You gain the **Juyo / Vaapad** stance for free, with its Guardian Mastery (a +2 circumstance bonus to attack rolls). While you're in it, after you take *or* deal damage, your next lightsaber Strike this round gains a +1 circumstance bonus to damage.

### Furious Intercede
**(3rd level)** When you Intercede, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to attack the triggering enemy, and your next Strike against it deals additional damage equal to your Strength modifier — you turn the fury of its blow back on it.

### Relentless Onslaught
**(7th level)** Once per round, after a lightsaber Strike hits, you can make an additional Strike at your current multiple attack penalty. You're then off-guard until the start of your next turn — the price of total commitment.

### Feed the Fury
**(11th level)** When an enemy hits you, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack and damage against it until the start of your next turn, and while in Juyo your attack bonus rises to +3 (your AC penalty stays −1).

### Unrelenting
**(15th level)** You ignore the off-guard condition caused by your own Juyo techniques, and your lightsaber critical hits make the target frightened 1.

### Avatar of Fury
**(19th level)** Once per round you gain an extra action you can use only for a lightsaber Strike, and while you're below half your Hit Points you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to attack and damage rolls.
