# Tech Specialist Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Tech Specialist class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a Discipline in their Prerequisites require it.

> **Sidebar — The Overclock & Rig Line**
> A cluster of feats shapes *how hard you can gamble and how much you can run*: the safety feats (Surge Protector, Recharge, Hardened Systems, Failsafe Protocols) soften the Overclock risk, and the rig feats (Expanded Rig, Master Engineer, Perfect Machine) add installed slots. The install feats **don't stack without limit** — bonus installs are capped at **+2 over your chassis count** (9 at most) — so your Standby passives stay an edge, not an avalanche.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Bypass
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Your damaging Modifications ignore the first 2 points of a target's Hardness or resistance (this increases as you gain levels).

### Efficient Rig
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Once per round, you can hot-swap an installed Modification as a single action rather than over 10 minutes.

### Field Improvisation
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
You can use Crafting to jury-rig temporary tools or consumables, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to skill checks a Modification supports.

### Reactive Countermeasure  [reaction]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Trigger** You're targeted by an attack.
**Effect** You deploy chaff or smoke, gaining concealment against the triggering attack (or a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against it).

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Diagnostic Sweep  [one-action]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
You run a multi-spectrum scan, Seeking a burst around you and revealing hidden technology and creatures.

### HoloNet Scavenger
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
You learn Modifications faster and more cheaply, and you can keep one extra Modification in your Library.

### Surge Protector
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Frequency** once per 10 minutes
**Effect** You treat an Overclock critical failure as an ordinary failure (no Modification is disabled).

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Armor-Piercing Protocols
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Your Modification and weapon damage ignores resistance equal to your Intelligence modifier against constructs and armored foes.

### Defibrillator Rounds
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Bio-Tech
Your healing Modifications work at a range of 30 feet.

### Reactive Shield  [reaction]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Trigger** You're hit by an attack.
**Effect** You magnetize debris and micro-deflectors, reducing the triggering attack's damage by 2 plus half your level.

### Recharge  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Frequency** once per 10 minutes
**Effect** You end your own Overclock lockout early.

### Armor-Shredder
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Armstech
When your rig-weapon Strike hits, the target takes a −1 circumstance penalty to AC until the start of your next turn.

### Combat Droid
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Droidwright
Your droid companion gains a second, stronger attack routine.

### System Crash
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Slicer
When you damage a foe's gear with a Modification, you can jam its weapon or blind one of its sensors as part of the effect.

### Unstable Conversion
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Scrapper
A Modification you've re-tuned with Jury-Rigged Adapter gains an added effect matching its new damage type (persistent damage, a push, a slow, and so on).

---

## 6th-Level Feats

### Expanded Rig
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
You can install one additional Modification. (Bonus installs from feats are capped at +2 over your chassis count.)

### Logic Bomb  [one-action]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Tech Specialist, Flourish
You launch a slicer strike that deals electricity damage and leaves the target clumsy 1 (off-guard on a critical hit).

### Suppressive Tech
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
A Modification you deploy can lay down difficult terrain or a pinning field across an area.

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Adaptive Countermeasures
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
During your daily preparations, choose a damage type; you gain resistance equal to your Intelligence modifier to it.

### Auto-Turret
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Armstech
You can deploy an autonomous turret — a Commanded emplacement that lays down fire when you Command it.

### Cannibalize
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Scrapper
You can strip a device or wrecked droid for a one-use salvaged part with an improvised effect.

### Dual Routine
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Once per round, you can Activate two single-action Modifications as a single two-action activity.

### Reinforced Droid
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Droidwright
Your droid gains additional durability and a defensive reaction.

### Sensor Lock
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
You designate a foe; you and your allies gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack rolls against it.

### Triage Protocol
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Bio-Tech
A healing Modification you Activate can split its healing between two allies.

### Viral Payload
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Slicer
Your Overload Logic spreads to an adjacent foe, dealing it the same effect.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Gravity Vortex  [two-actions]
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Tech Specialist, Flourish
You overcharge a gravity-inverter: a burst that pulls foes toward its center and deals bludgeoning damage (basic Reflex against your Tech Specialist class DC).

### Overcharged Core
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Your rig self-sustains: at the start of each of your turns, if your last Overclock only *failed* (not critically failed), you can Overclock again.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Ablative Mastery
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Scrapper
Your Ablative Casing works twice as often (once per 10 minutes becomes twice per 10 minutes).

### Cascade Overclock
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
When you succeed at an Overclock flat check, a minor secondary effect triggers — a free Stride, or a 5-foot push on a creature affected.

### Hardened Systems
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
An Overclock critical failure no longer disables the Modification (you still can't Overclock for 10 minutes).

### Heavy Ordnance Expert
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Armstech
Your area Modifications and weapons gain an additional die and a larger area.

### Miracle Nanites
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Bio-Tech
**Frequency** once per 10 minutes
**Effect** You release self-replicating nanites; an ally gains fast healing for a short time.

### Networked Command
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Droidwright
Once per round, you can Command your droid as a free action.

### Total Override
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Slicer
**Frequency** once per 10 minutes
**Effect** You Slice several devices or constructs at once.

---

## 14th-Level Feats

### Master Engineer
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Prerequisites** Expanded Rig
You can install one additional Modification (within the +2-over-chassis cap), and once per round you can reduce a Modification's Activate cost by one action.

### Repulsor Field  [reaction]
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Trigger** A creature adjacent to you attacks you.
**Effect** A localized blast pushes the attacker 5 feet, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack.

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Failsafe Protocols
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
The first Overclock you attempt in each combat automatically succeeds at its flat check.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Experimental Core
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Your Overclock effects deal an additional die beyond their normal boost.

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### Apotheosis Engine
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You Activate a Modification as a free action.

### Perfect Machine
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Tech Specialist
Your Overclock flat check can't be a critical failure, and you can install one additional Modification (within the +2-over-chassis cap).
