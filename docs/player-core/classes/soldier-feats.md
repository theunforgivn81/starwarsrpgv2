# Soldier Feats

At every even level — and 1st — you gain a **Soldier class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a **fighting style** in their Prerequisites require that style.

> **Sidebar — Spending Your One [Suppressed] Effect**
> Feats tagged **[Suppressed]** add an effect you can attach when you make a creature suppressed — but only **one** [Suppressed] effect applies per suppressing action (see *Suppressing Fire*). Collecting several gives you options to pick between, not a stack. The only exception is **Double Suppression**, below.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Brace  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Soldier
Until the start of your next turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC and can't be moved against your will.

### Demand Attention  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Soldier, Emotion, Mental
One enemy within 30 feet attempts a Will save against your Soldier class DC.
**Critical Success** The target is unaffected.
**Success** Until the start of your next turn, the target takes a –1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls that don't include you.
**Failure** As Success, but the penalty lasts 1 minute or until the target hits you, whichever comes first.
**Critical Failure** As Failure, and the target is **off-guard** until the start of your next turn.

### Knockback Blast
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Soldier, Suppressed
When you make a creature suppressed with Suppressing Fire, you can choose this effect: each creature that fails its save is pushed 5 feet directly away from you.

### Suppressing Salvo
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Soldier
Immediately after you use Suppressing Fire, you can make a single ranged Strike against a creature in the affected area. This Strike benefits from Overwhelming Assault's penalty reduction even against a target that isn't yet suppressed.

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Covering Fire  [reaction]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Soldier
**Trigger** An enemy in your Suppression Zone attacks one of your allies.
**Effect** The triggering enemy takes a –1 circumstance penalty to that attack roll.

### Field Reload
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Soldier
Once per turn, you can Interact to reload a weapon you're wielding as a free action.

### Run, Cowards!
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Soldier, Suppressed
When you make a creature suppressed with Suppressing Fire, you can choose this effect: each creature that **critically fails** its save is fleeing until the end of its next turn.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Armored Reaction  [reaction]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Bulwark
**Trigger** You're hit by a Strike.
**Effect** You gain resistance to that Strike's damage equal to half your level (equal to your level if the attacker is suppressed). Then you can Shove the attacker if it's within reach.

### Bring It Down
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
Your Strikes against a suppressed foe deal one additional weapon damage die.

### Cleaving Sweep
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Vanguard
When you make a melee Strike, you can also make a melee Strike against a second foe in your reach. This second Strike uses the same multiple attack penalty as the first.

### Immobilizing Fire
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier, Suppressed
When you make a creature suppressed with Suppressing Fire, you can choose this effect: each creature that fails its save is immobilized until it Escapes (against your Soldier class DC).

### Indiscriminate
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Suppressor
When you Auto-Fire, you can shape it as a 10-foot emanation centered on yourself, blanketing every direction at once instead of a cone.

### Leg Shot
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier, Suppressed
When you make a creature suppressed, you can choose this effect: each affected creature is off-guard until the start of your next turn.

### Wide Barrage
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
Increase the area of your Suppressing Fire (and any other area attack you make) by 5 feet — a longer cone or a wider burst.

### Wide Blast
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Demolitionist
Your area attacks are larger, and you can exclude additional allies from them equal to your Constitution modifier.

### Withering Glare
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Enforcer
Your Oppressive Presence affects two foes at once, or a single foe at up to 120 feet.

## 6th-Level Feats

### Body Block  [reaction]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Soldier
**Trigger** An enemy hits an ally adjacent to you with a Strike.
**Effect** You become the target of that Strike instead, applying the result against your AC rather than your ally's.

### Cratering Rounds
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Soldier
When you make an area attack, the ground in the affected area becomes difficult terrain until the start of your next turn.

### Reckless Barrage  [one-action]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Soldier
Until the start of your next turn, you take a –2 circumstance penalty to AC and gain resistance to all damage equal to half your level.

### Scattering Fire
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Soldier
When you use Suppressing Fire, you can also scatter those caught in it: each creature that **fails** its save against that Suppressing Fire is pushed **10 feet** directly away from you, and each creature that **critically fails** is also knocked **prone**.

## 8th-Level Feats

### Air Burst
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Suppressor
Your Suppression Zone and Suppressing Reaction reach and pin flying creatures, and your fire ignores lesser cover in the zone.

### Collateral Witness
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
Immediately after you use Suppressing Fire, you can Demoralize one creature in the affected area as a free action.

### Combat Reflexes
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
You gain an additional reaction each round, which you can use only for your Suppressing Reaction, Covering Fire, or a Reactive Shot.

> **Sidebar — Your Reactions Have a Ceiling**
> No matter how many reactions your feats and features grant, you can use at most **three reactions per round**. Combat Reflexes, Withering Fire, and Greater Combat Reflexes raise you *toward* that ceiling, never past it.

### Field Tactician
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Enforcer
Allies gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack rolls against any foe you've menaced with Oppressive Presence this turn.

### Immovable Object
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Bulwark
You're immune to forced movement and to being knocked prone, and you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Dig In resistance while adjacent to a suppressed foe.

### Reactive Swing  [reaction]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Vanguard
**Trigger** A creature moves into a square within your reach.
**Effect** Make a melee Strike against the triggering creature, even with an unwieldy weapon.

### Smoke Screen
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
After you use Suppressing Fire, you're concealed until the start of your next turn.

### Sticky Charge
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Demolitionist
A creature that fails its save against your area attack takes persistent damage of the weapon's type and is marked for your next area attack, which automatically includes it if able.

### Two-Pronged Salvo
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Soldier
Your Suppressing Salvo can target two creatures in the area instead of one.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Get Back Up
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Soldier
When you lose the dying condition, you gain temporary Hit Points equal to your level until the end of the encounter.

### Hybrid Technique
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Soldier
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You can combine two of your single-action Soldier abilities into a single two-action activity, performing both in either order.

### Last Stand  [reaction]
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Soldier
**Trigger** You're reduced to 0 Hit Points.
**Effect** Before you fall, make one ranged or melee Strike.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Bunker Buster
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Demolitionist
Your area attacks ignore cover and reduce the Hardness of objects and barriers in the area by your Constitution modifier.

### Ceaseless Barrage
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Suppressor
On your turn, you can both maintain your Suppression Zone and make a ranged Strike, rather than choosing between them.

### Cowed
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Enforcer
A creature that ends the suppression of your Oppressive Presence (by acting against you) is frightened 1 as it does so.

### Crushing Grapple
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Bulwark
A creature you have grabbed or restrained takes damage equal to your Constitution modifier at the start of each of your turns and remains suppressed for as long as you hold it.

### Overwhelming Barrage
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
Your Auto-Fire and Area Fire attacks gain one additional damage die, and their areas increase by 5 feet.

### Repulsor Blast  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
You unleash a concussive cone (15 feet). Each creature in the area attempts a basic Reflex save against your Soldier class DC.
**Critical Success** The creature is unaffected.
**Success** The creature takes half damage.
**Failure** The creature takes full damage, is pushed 10 feet, and falls prone.
**Critical Failure** The creature takes double damage, is pushed 20 feet, and falls prone.

### Rocket Jump  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Requirements** You're wielding a weapon with the area trait.
**Effect** Fire downward to launch yourself; Leap up to half your weapon's first range increment in any direction, ignoring intervening ground.

### Rotary Onslaught  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Vanguard
Make a melee Strike against each creature in your reach, and each creature you hit becomes suppressed.

## 14th-Level Feats

### Double Suppression
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Soldier
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** When you make creatures suppressed, you can apply **two** [Suppressed] effects on that action instead of one.

> **Example — Stacking the Effects**
> *Master Sergeant Ko lays down Suppressing Fire and, with Double Suppression, attaches both Leg Shot (off-guard) and Run, Cowards! (fleeing on a critical failure). A droid that critically fails is now off-guard **and** fleeing — two [Suppressed] effects from one barrage.*

### Unflinching
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Soldier
The first time you're hit each round, reduce the value of one condition affecting you by 1.

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Greater Combat Reflexes
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Soldier
**Prerequisites** Combat Reflexes
You gain a further reaction each round (up to the three-reaction limit), and your Suppressing Reaction can target two creatures with a single use.

### Indomitable
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Soldier
**Frequency** once per 10 minutes
**Effect** When you fail a saving throw, you can treat it as a success instead.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Air Superiority
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Soldier
Your suppression reaches and pins flying foes anywhere in your zone, and your fire ignores all cover within it.

### Withering Onslaught
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Soldier
Your Suppressing Fire is overwhelming: a creature that **succeeds** at its save still takes full damage (only a critical success halves it).

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### Bullet Typhoon
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Soldier
You can Auto-Fire or Area Fire as a single action. You can make at most two such attacks per round.

### Living Legend
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Soldier
Your Suppressing Fire features work with heavy weapons and melee weapons at the same time, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to attack rolls while any foe you've menaced this combat remains in the fight.
