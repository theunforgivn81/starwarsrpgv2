# Scoundrel Specializations

At 1st level, every Scoundrel chooses a **specialization** — the discipline that steers your Exploit and decides how you make a foe exploitable in the first place. It grants a signature skill and a feature at **1st level**, then further features at **3rd, 7th, 11th, 15th, and 19th**.

Choose one of the five below when you make your Scoundrel. You can't change it later except through specific feats.

---

# Smuggler

*The Smuggler talks first and shoots second — usually because the talking already won.* You open foes up with a lie, a wink, or a leveled blaster and a friendly smile.

### Silver Tongue

**(1st level)** You become trained in **Deception**, plus your choice of **Diplomacy** or **Intimidation**. Your patter is a weapon: your **Feint** is a single action, and on a success the target is **off-guard to you until the end of your next turn** — a full Exploit window, not just one shot. You can Feint at range while wielding a gun, using a quip or feigned shot in place of a blade's flick.

### Disarming Charm

**(3rd level)** After you succeed at a Feint or to Make an Impression on a creature, it takes a **–1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls against you** until the start of your next turn.

### Sell the Lie

**(7th level)** Once per round, a foe you make off-guard with Feint is **off-guard to your allies** as well, not just to you.

### Con Artist

**(11th level)** Your Feint can target **two** foes at once, and your Exploit against a foe you've Feinted this turn gains **one additional die**.

### Smooth Operator

**(15th level)** You're a legend in any cantina — a master of false identities, signature appeals, and talking past security. In combat, once per round you can **Feint as a free action**.

### Master Manipulator

**(19th level)** *Capstone.* A foe you Feint is **off-guard to everyone** until the start of your next turn, and you can plant a command in a mark's mind.

**Suggest**  [two-actions]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, linguistic, mental, [once per minute]
**Effect** One creature within 30 feet that can understand you attempts a Will save against your Scoundrel class DC.
**Critical Success** The target is unaffected and knows you tried to manipulate it.
**Success** The target is unaffected.
**Failure** The target follows a suggested course of action (one that isn't obviously self-destructive) for 1 round.
**Critical Failure** As Failure, but for up to 1 minute or until the suggestion would clearly endanger it.

# Sniper

*The Sniper is a problem solved from a distance the enemy can't answer.* One mark, one breath, one shot that was already a certainty.

### Marksman

**(1st level)** You become trained in **Stealth**. You ignore the **unwieldy** and **volley** traits of sniper-group weapons, and your **Aim** can Mark a target at **any range** — your Exploit and cover reduction apply even beyond your weapon's first range increment.

### Keep Them in Your Sights

**(3rd level)** You don't lose your Mark when it briefly leaves your line of sight, and you gain a **+1 circumstance bonus** to attack a Marked foe more than 60 feet away.

### One Shot

**One Shot**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Requirements** You have a Mark.
**(7th level)** **Effect** Make a single ranged Strike against your Mark. It gains **one additional Exploit die**, and if you haven't moved this turn, it ignores the Mark's cover and concealment.

### Deadeye

**(11th level)** Your Exploit against your Mark **ignores all cover**, and your critical hits against your Mark gain the weapon's critical specialization effect.

### Overwatch

**Overwatch**  [reaction]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** Your Mark makes a Strike, uses a manifestation or device, or leaves its space.
**(15th level)** **Effect** Make a ranged Strike against your Mark.

### Perfect Shot

**Perfect Shot**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [once per minute]
**Requirements** You have a Mark.
**(19th level)** *Capstone.* **Effect** Make a ranged Strike against your Mark. If it hits, it's a **critical hit**.

# Gunslinger

*The Gunslinger fights where it's loudest — in the enemy's face, both pistols speaking.* Speed and nerve where others want distance.

### Point-Blank

**(1st level)** You become trained in **Acrobatics**. Your one-handed ranged Strikes don't trigger reactions that key off ranged attacks, so you can fire in melee freely, and the first time you ready a one-handed gun in an encounter, you **Aim as a free action**.

### Deadly Aim

**(3rd level)** When you Strike an **adjacent** foe with a one-handed gun, treat the lowest die of that weapon's damage as its maximum value (the two lowest dice at 17th level).

### Trick Shot

**(7th level)** You learn three trick shots, each a separate **[one-action]** **[Flourish]** activity. (If you already have a feat granting one of these, you instead learn another trick shot of your choice.)

**Disarming Shot**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Effect** Make a ranged Strike that also attempts to knock a held item from the target; on a hit, the target attempts a save against your Scoundrel class DC or drops one held item.

**Ricochet Shot**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Effect** Make a ranged Strike that ignores the target's cover and concealment, banked off a nearby surface.

**Pinning Shot**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Effect** Make a ranged Strike; on a hit, the target is **off-guard** until the start of your next turn.

### Rapid Fire

**Rapid Fire**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per round
**(11th level)** **Effect** Make two ranged Strikes, against the same target or two targets within 10 feet of each other. Both use your current multiple attack penalty, which doesn't increase until after the activity.

### Fastest Draw

**(15th level)** You gain a **+2 circumstance bonus** to initiative rolls, and you're always ready to clear leather.

**Quick Draw**  [reaction]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** A creature within 30 feet draws a weapon or makes a Strike.
**Effect** Draw a one-handed gun if needed and make a ranged Strike against the triggering creature.

### Hail of Fire

**Hail of Fire**  [two-actions]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [once per minute]
**(19th level)** *Capstone.* **Effect** Make a ranged Strike against each enemy in a 15-foot burst within range, each with its own attack roll and MAP shared across them. Each Strike against an exploitable foe is an Exploit.

# Infiltrator

*The Infiltrator was never supposed to be here, and won't be by the time anyone checks.* Stealth, slicing, and the shot from a shadow.

### Ghost

**(1st level)** You become trained in **Stealth** and **Computers**, and a creature that is **unaware of you** is exploitable to you. You also gain the Sabotage action.

**Sabotage**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, technological
**Effect** Attempt a Computers check against a technological creature's or device's DC. On a success, the target is **glitching** until the start of your next turn (taking a status penalty to its checks and DCs); on a critical success, it's also briefly disabled.

### Ghost Tap

**Ghost Tap**  [free-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** You make a Strike while hidden or undetected.
**(3rd level)** **Effect** Attempt a Deception check to immediately become hidden again from the target, even though striking would normally reveal you.

### Sabotage Mastery

**(7th level)** Your Sabotage gains greater range and effect; you can slice a droid or device into malfunctioning for a full round, fouling its actions.

### Phantom

**(11th level)** Your Exploit while you're hidden or undetected gains **one additional die**, and once per round you can **Hide even while observed**, slipping out of sight in plain view.

### Infiltrator's Craft

**(15th level)** You're a master of disguise and false credentials, reliably bypassing security checks. Foes you Sabotage are **off-guard to everyone** until the start of your next turn.

### Untraceable

**Untraceable**  [free-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [once per minute]
**(19th level)** *Capstone.* **Effect** You become **undetected** by all creatures until you act. If you make a Strike from undetected before the end of your turn, it deals **maximum Exploit dice**.

# Striker

*The Striker closes the distance and ends it — a vibroblade where everyone expected a blaster.* The Scoundrel's precision, in melee.

### Close-Quarters Operator

**(1st level)** You become trained in **Athletics**, and you become **expert in finesse and agile melee weapons and unarmed attacks**. Uniquely, these advance alongside your ranged sub-track — your finesse melee reaches **legendary** when your guns do. You can use **Aim with a finesse melee weapon** to set a melee Mark, and you gain Reactive Step.

**Reactive Step**  [reaction]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** A creature moves into a space within your reach.
**Effect** Step toward the triggering creature.

### Overwhelming Strike

**(3rd level)** Once per turn, when your Strike hits a foe that's exploitable to you, that foe becomes **enfeebled 1**, **off-guard**, or **clumsy 1** (your choice) until the start of your next turn.

### Flurry

**Flurry**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**(7th level)** **Effect** Make two melee Strikes with a finesse or agile weapon. Both use your current multiple attack penalty, which doesn't increase until after the activity.

### Tumbling Assault

**Tumbling Assault**  [two-actions]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel
**(11th level)** **Effect** Stride up to your Speed. During this movement you can move through one enemy's space (attempt Acrobatics to Tumble Through against its Reflex DC). Make one melee Strike against that enemy at any point during the movement; the enemy is off-guard to this Strike.

### Lethal Precision

**(15th level)** Your melee Exploit gains **one additional die**, and you gain the critical specialization effect of finesse melee weapons.

### Assassinate

**Assassinate**  [one-action]  **·  Traits** Scoundrel, [once per minute]
**Requirements** Your target is off-guard to you or unaware of you.
**(19th level)** *Capstone.* **Effect** Make a melee Strike against the target. It deals **double Exploit dice**, and the target must succeed at a Fortitude save against your Scoundrel class DC or be **slowed 1** until the start of your next turn.
