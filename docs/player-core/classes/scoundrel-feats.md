# Scoundrel Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Scoundrel class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a **specialization** in their Prerequisites require it.

> **Sidebar — Flourishes and Reactions**
> Many trick shots carry the **[Flourish]** trait — you can use only **one Flourish action per round**, so a deep trick-shot menu is a set of *choices*, not a combo. Separately, your **[reaction]** feats (Nimble Dodge, Gun Parry, Combat Roll, Defensive Roll) all compete for your single baseline reaction; like every character, you can use at most **three reactions per round**.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Coordinated Fire
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Scoundrel
A foe that one of your allies is adjacent to or flanking is exploitable to you, even at range.

### Disarming Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a ranged Strike against your Mark that also attempts to disarm it; on a hit, the target attempts a save against your Scoundrel class DC or drops one held item.

### Mobile Aim
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per round
You can Aim as part of a Stride, setting up on the move.

### Nimble Dodge  [reaction]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** A creature you can see targets you with an attack.
**Effect** You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against that attack.

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Quick Swap
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Once per turn, you can draw, stow, or swap a weapon as a free action.

### Ricochet Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a ranged Strike that ignores the target's cover and concealment, banked off a nearby surface.

### Sidestep
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per round
Immediately after you make a Strike, you can Step as a free action.

### Vital Shot
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Your Exploit against your Mark also deals persistent bleed damage equal to the size of one Exploit die.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Crippling Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a ranged Strike against your Mark; on a hit, the Mark is **clumsy 1** or takes a –10-foot status penalty to its Speeds (your choice) until the start of your next turn.

### Deep Slice
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Infiltrator
You can use Sabotage on doors and security systems, and disable a device at a distance.

### Double Tap  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make two ranged Strikes against a single target. Both use your current multiple attack penalty, which doesn't increase until after the activity.

### Fast Talk
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Smuggler
Your Feint can target two foes, or a single foe up to 30 feet away.

### Knockout Blow
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Striker
When you hit a foe that's off-guard to you with a melee Strike, it must succeed at a Fortitude save against your Scoundrel class DC or be **stunned 1**.

### Long Shot
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Sniper
Your Aim and Exploit function out to your weapon's **fourth** range increment.

### Mobile Strike  [two-actions]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Stride up to your Speed and make a ranged Strike at any point during the movement.

### Piercing Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a single ranged Strike against each creature in a line, using one attack roll against each.

### Twin Pistols
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Gunslinger
While wielding two one-handed guns, you can reload both with a single Interact action.

## 6th-Level Feats

### Finisher
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Scoundrel
When you reduce a foe to 0 HP, or reduce your Mark below half its HP, you can Aim at a new target as a free action.

### Gun Parry  [reaction]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** You're targeted by a melee attack.
**Effect** Interpose a wielded gun to gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against that attack.

### Skirmisher's Step
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Your movement doesn't trigger reactions from any creature you've Struck this turn.

### Spray  [two-actions]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Scoundrel
You rake a 15-foot cone with fire. Each creature in the area attempts a basic Reflex save against your Scoundrel class DC, taking your weapon's damage.
**Critical Success** Unaffected. **Success** Half damage. **Failure** Full damage. **Critical Failure** Double damage.

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Bamboozle
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Smuggler
A foe you make off-guard with Feint can't use reactions until the start of your next turn.

### Called Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a precise ranged Strike against your Mark; on a hit, the Mark is **off-guard** until the start of your next turn.

### Combat Roll  [reaction]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Trigger** You would be subject to an effect that allows a basic Reflex save.
**Effect** Step before attempting the save.

### Fan the Hammer  [one-action]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Prerequisites** Gunslinger
Make three ranged Strikes against a single adjacent foe with a one-handed gun. All use your current multiple attack penalty, which doesn't increase until after the activity.

### No Cover
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Sniper
Your Mark gains no benefit from concealment against your attacks.

### Smoke Bomb  [one-action]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Infiltrator
You drop a concealing cloud in a 10-foot burst and can immediately Hide.

### Twin Guns
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Scoundrel
While wielding two one-handed guns, your Aim applies to Strikes you make with either of them.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Deadly Precision
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Scoundrel
The first Strike you make each turn against an exploitable foe gains **one additional Exploit die**.

### Living Weapon
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Scoundrel
You can't be made off-guard at the start of an encounter while you're armed, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to initiative.

### Run and Gun  [two-actions]
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Stride up to your Speed, then make two ranged Strikes. Both Strikes use your current multiple attack penalty, which doesn't increase until after the activity.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Assassin's Mark
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Infiltrator
A Strike you make from undetected against your Mark gains **two additional Exploit dice**.

### Defensive Roll  [reaction]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per minute
**Trigger** You're hit by an attack that would reduce you to 0 HP.
**Effect** Halve that attack's damage.

### Death Blossom  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Prerequisites** Striker
Make a melee Strike with a finesse or agile weapon against each foe adjacent to you, sharing one multiple attack penalty.

### Killing Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make an aimed ranged Strike against your Mark with **one additional Exploit die**; on a critical hit, the Mark is **slowed 1** until the start of your next turn.

### Quickest Draw
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Gunslinger
At the start of an encounter, before initiative is rolled, you can Aim and make one ranged Strike.

### Slip the Net  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
**Prerequisites** Smuggler
Attempt to Escape or to vanish into a crowd; you can also talk your way out of a grab, using Deception against the grabber's DC.

### Through the Scope
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Prerequisites** Sniper
You never lose your Mark to line of sight, and your critical hit range against your Mark increases by 1 (you crit on a 19).

### Whirlwind Fire  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Scoundrel, [Flourish]
Make a ranged Strike against up to three foes within range, sharing one multiple attack penalty.

## 14th-Level Feats

### Greater Trick Shot
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Your [Flourish] trick shots deal **one additional die** of damage when they hit.

### Untouchable
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Any foe you've Struck this turn takes a –1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Perfect Aim
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Your Exploit against your Mark ignores the target's resistance to precision damage.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Unerring Shot  [one-action]
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per minute
Make a ranged Strike that automatically hits (this isn't a critical hit).

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### Deadeye Legend
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Scoundrel
**Frequency** once per round
Maximize the Exploit dice on one Strike.

### One in a Million
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Scoundrel
Your Strikes against an exploitable foe are critical hits on a 19–20.
