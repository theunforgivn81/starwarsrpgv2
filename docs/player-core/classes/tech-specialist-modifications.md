# Tech Specialist Modifications

These are the **Modifications** — the Tech Specialist's toolbox. You learn them into your **Library** and install a subset into your **Custom Rig** (see *Deploy*, in the [Tech Specialist](tech-specialist.md) class). Unlike the Force toolkit, Modifications are yours alone; they aren't a shared chapter.

**Reading a Modification.** Every entry has the same parts:
- **Modification [N]** — the **level** at which you can learn and install it.
- **Standby** — the always-on passive while the Modification is installed.
- **Activate** — the active effect and its action cost. Activating is **at-will**. Save effects are rolled against your **Tech Specialist class DC**.
- **Overclock** — the boosted version: bigger dice, **plus your Intelligence modifier**, and usually an added condition or area. After resolving it, attempt the **Overclock flat check** (DC 15; see *Deploy*).

**Scaling.** A damage Modification gains **+1 die at 5th, 11th, and 17th levels**, like a Force power. The base Activate adds no attribute; only an Overclock adds your Intelligence. Strong single-target heals and stims can affect a given target only **once per 10 minutes**.

## Offense Modifications

### Overload Logic · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Electricity
**Standby** You can wirelessly Slice or Disable a Device at up to 15 feet.
**Activate** [two-actions] One creature within 30 feet takes 1d6 electricity damage (basic Reflex).
**Overclock** Step the damage die to d8 and add your Intelligence modifier; the target is also clumsy 1 until the start of your next turn.

### Fusion Torch · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Fire
**Standby** You gain a +1 item bonus to Force Open metal barriers.
**Activate** Make a melee plasma Strike (fire and slashing, finesse) that ignores an amount of Hardness equal to your level.
**Overclock** Gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the attack, step up the damage dice, and ignore additional Hardness.

### Repulsor Emitter · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** Increase your Bulk limits by 2.
**Activate** [two-actions] A 15-foot cone deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage and pushes those who fail 5 feet (basic Fortitude).
**Overclock** Becomes a 10-foot emanation, adds your Intelligence modifier, and knocks failures prone.

### Fragmentation Charge · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You keep a charge primed and ready to throw.
**Activate** [two-actions] Throw a frag charge; a burst within 30 feet deals 1d6 piercing damage (basic Reflex).
**Overclock** Larger burst, add your Intelligence modifier, and failures take persistent bleed damage.

### Endothermic Vent · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Cold
**Standby** You gain cold protection (resistance to environmental cold).
**Activate** [two-actions] A cone deals 1d6 cold damage and slows failures (basic Fortitude).
**Overclock** Add your Intelligence modifier; a failure is instead immobilized until the end of its next turn.

### Corrosive Solvent · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Acid
**Standby** +2 item bonus to Pick a Lock or Escape using the solvent.
**Activate** [two-actions] A 20-foot acid cone deals 2d6 acid damage (basic Reflex).
**Overclock** Step the die to d8, add your Intelligence modifier, and failures take persistent acid damage and are clumsy 1.

### Plasma Incinerator · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Fire
**Standby** You're immune to environmental cold and can ignite materials at a touch.
**Activate** [two-actions] A 30-foot line deals 2d6 fire damage (basic Reflex).
**Overclock** Extend to a 60-foot line, add your Intelligence modifier, and deal persistent fire damage to failures.

### Sonic Resonator · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Sonic
**Standby** You gain echolocation out to 30 feet (an imprecise sense).
**Activate** [two-actions] One creature takes 2d8 sonic damage (basic Fortitude); on a critical failure it's deafened.
**Overclock** Step the die to d10, add your Intelligence modifier, and a failure is deafened and stunned 1.

## Healing & Support Modifications

### Stim-Injector · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** +1 item bonus to saves against fatigue, and you need half the usual sleep.
**Activate** [two-actions] Touch a creature to grant it temporary Hit Points and a +10-foot status bonus to Speed for 1 minute (once per target per 10 minutes).
**Overclock** Larger temporary Hit Points plus your Intelligence modifier, and the target can immediately Step.

### Kolto Burst · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Healing
**Standby** +1 item bonus to Administer First Aid; if you'd critically fail to Treat Wounds, you get a failure instead.
**Activate** [two-actions] Touch a creature to restore 1d8 Hit Points (once per target per 10 minutes).
**Overclock** Step the die to d12, add your Intelligence modifier, and end one persistent bleed.

### Defibrillation Matrix · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Healing
**Standby** You read the Hit Points and conditions of allies within 30 feet, and gain +2 to Treat Disease or Poison.
**Activate** [two-actions] Touch a creature to restore 2d8 Hit Points and reduce its dying value by 1 (once per target per 10 minutes).
**Overclock** Step the dice up and add your Intelligence modifier; you can instead **revive** a creature that died within the last round.

### Nanite Reconstruction Swarm · Modification 15
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Healing
**Standby** A faint repair-swarm grants you fast healing 2 while you're at half Hit Points or below.
**Activate** [two-actions] Release a nanite cloud in a 15-foot burst; allies within regain 3d8 Hit Points over the next few rounds.
**Overclock** Add your Intelligence modifier to the healing, and the swarm also counteracts one condition or grants brief regeneration.

## Defense Modifications

### Deflector Screen · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You gain protection from harsh weather and environmental hazards.
**Activate** [one-action] Gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC until the start of your next turn.
**Overclock** The bonus is +2, and a creature that hits you in melee takes electricity damage equal to your Intelligence modifier.

### Hard-Light Bastion · Modification 15
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You can erect a secure shelter for resting.
**Activate** [three-actions] Deploy a 20-foot hard-light bunker providing cover (Hardness 20, 80 Hit Points).
**Overclock** The bunker has Hardness 30, and creatures adjacent to its exterior take electricity damage when it forms.

## Control Modifications

### Gravitic Snare · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You ignore difficult terrain from rubble and uneven ground.
**Activate** [two-actions] A 10-foot burst within 60 feet deals 2d6 bludgeoning damage and becomes difficult terrain (basic Reflex).
**Overclock** Add your Intelligence modifier; a failure is immobilized, and a critical failure is restrained (Escape against your Tech Specialist class DC).

### Flash-Bang Matrix · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You're resistant to light-based and flash effects.
**Activate** [two-actions] A burst dazzles those who fail and blinds those who critically fail (basic save against your Tech Specialist class DC).
**Overclock** Larger area, add your Intelligence modifier, and a failure is stunned 1.

### Neural Override · Modification 15
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Incapacitation
**Standby** You can commune with constructs (machine empathy).
**Activate** [two-actions] Hijack a construct or overwhelm a mind within range — control or stun it (save against your Tech Specialist class DC).
**Overclock** The control or stun is longer or stronger.

## Utility, Mobility & Stealth Modifications

### Holo-Projector · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist, Illusion
**Standby** +1 item bonus to Create a Diversion and to Impersonate.
**Activate** [two-actions] Project a hard-light decoy within 30 feet (disbelieved with a check against your Tech Specialist class DC).
**Overclock** The decoy flares blindingly: creatures near it take a −1 status penalty to ranged attacks and Perception.

### Sensory Baffle · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You can hear whispers through ambient noise.
**Activate** [two-actions] Create a 15-foot sound-dampening field that grants a bonus to Stealth.
**Overclock** Larger bonus, and creatures inside gain sonic resistance equal to your Intelligence modifier.

### Telemetry Uplink · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You gain passive battlefield awareness (a bonus to initiative and to notice threats).
**Activate** [one-action] Mark and scan a foe, sharing targeting data so your next attack against it benefits.
**Overclock** The targeting data is shared party-wide against that foe.

### Magnetic Grapple · Modification 1
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You gain a climb Speed on metal surfaces.
**Activate** [one-action] Pull an unattended object to your hand from 30 feet, or pull yourself to a metal structure (this doesn't trigger reactions).
**Overclock** You can grapple a creature instead (an Athletics check using your Intelligence), pulling it to you or you to it.

### Aero-Booster · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** Treat falls as 20 feet shorter.
**Activate** [one-action] Fly up to your Speed on a jump-pack burst.
**Overclock** Gain a fly Speed for 5 minutes.

### Optical Cloak · Modification 7
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** +2 item bonus to Hide and Sneak in dim light.
**Activate** [two-actions] Become undetected to sight for 5 minutes (ending if you take a hostile action).
**Overclock** Lasts 1 minute but survives your first hostile action (you become hidden rather than observed afterward).

### Quantum Displacement Field · Modification 15
**Traits** Tech Specialist
**Standby** You can Interact with objects at a range of 15 feet.
**Activate** [two-actions] Teleport yourself and up to four willing allies within 60 feet to a location you can see.
**Overclock** The spaces you vacate implode, dealing bludgeoning damage plus your Intelligence modifier (basic Fortitude) to creatures there.
