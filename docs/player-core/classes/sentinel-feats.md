# Sentinel Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Sentinel class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a fork in their Prerequisites require that fork.

> **Sidebar — The Conduit Feats**
> A line of feats (Reactive Conduit, Deflecting Conduit, Force Reflection Conduit, Deep Conduit, Greater Conduit) lets you channel **Force powers from *The Force*** — especially Sense and Control reactions like Precognitive Parry, Energy Absorption, and Force Reflection — *through* your Premonition engine, cheaper and sharper. They don't make those powers yours alone; they make you the specialist who uses them best.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Danger Sense
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Sentinel
You can't be made off-guard by creatures you've detected, and you gain a +2 circumstance bonus against being ambushed or surprised.

### Empathic Read
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Sentinel
You can use Perception to read a creature's intentions and emotions, gaining a +1 circumstance bonus to sense lies and hostility.

### Foretelling Step
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Sentinel
When you use Foretell, you can Step.

### Watchful Initiative
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Sentinel
You roll Perception for initiative with a +2 circumstance bonus, and you can't be off-guard during the first round of combat.

---

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Anticipatory Reflex
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You use a defensive Premonition response (Evade or Warn) even when you have no reaction remaining, by spending 1 Attunement.

### Evasive Foresight
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your **Evade** Step is 10 feet and ignores difficult terrain.

### Premonitory Warning
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your **Warn** response can protect two allies within range from the same triggering effect.

### Quick Draw
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Sentinel
You can draw or switch a weapon as part of a Strike or a Counter.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Ambush Reflex
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Shadow
When you Counter from concealment, you can Step before making the Strike.

### Disorienting Counter
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
When your **Counter** hits, the target can't make you off-guard until the start of your next turn.

### Foreseen Pivot
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You reroll a failed saving throw against a creature you can perceive, keeping the higher result.

### Punishing Resolve
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Justicar
After a foe damages you, your next **Counter** against it deals an additional 2 status damage.

### Quickened Foresight
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
Foretell is a free action the first time each round you roll initiative or a foreseen foe falls.

### Reactive Conduit
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
A Force power with the Sense or Control trait that you use as a reaction costs 1 less Attunement (minimum 0).

### Tactical Analysis
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Investigator
When you share a Recalled fact, allies gain a +1 circumstance bonus against that foe (instead of just one ally).

### Flashy Feint
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Corsair
Your Underhanded Trick works at 30 feet even unarmed, and a creature you feint is off-guard to your allies too.

---

## 6th-Level Feats

### Anticipatory Strike
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your first Strike against a creature you Foretold this turn treats it as off-guard.

### Guardian's Reflex
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your free defensive response (Evade or Warn) can also grant an adjacent ally cover against the triggering attack.

### Mind Fortress
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Sentinel
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Will saves, and once per round you can reduce your frightened or fascinated value by 1 as a free action.

### Opportune Counter
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your **Counter** can also trigger when a creature in reach Strides or makes a ranged attack (still once per round).

### Precognitive Dodge
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** When you would be critically hit, spend 1 Attunement to make it a normal hit instead.

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Blindsense
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
You gain an imprecise sense out to 15 feet, and you ignore concealment from creatures within it.

### Deadly Foresight
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Shadow
Your Shadow precision die increases by 1d6.

### Deflecting Conduit
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
You add **Energy Absorption** to your repertoire (see *The Force*); using it counts as a Premonition (fired through your engine).

### Pressure Point
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Investigator
Your **Maneuver** can Grapple, and a creature you've grabbed is off-guard to your Counter.

### Ricochet Stratagem
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Corsair
Your ranged Strikes ignore the Concealed condition and standard cover (but not greater cover).

### Shared Premonition
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
When you Foretell a foe, one ally treats it as off-guard on their next attack against it.

### Wide-Angle Sight
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your Premonition range increases to 60 feet, and you can perceive triggers through sense-blunting conditions.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Deterring Presence
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Justicar
Foes you've Countered this encounter take a −1 circumstance penalty to attack you.

### Extended Guard
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Warden
Your Interpose range increases to 15 feet, and you can Step to the ally as part of it.

### Force Reflection Conduit
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Sentinel
You add **Force Reflection** to your repertoire (see *The Force*), fired as a Premonition (a ranged redirect).

### Storm of Premonition
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** This round, your Overdrive Premonition costs no Attunement.

### Unshakable
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Danger Sense
You can't be made off-guard by flanking, by being surprised, or by creatures hidden from you.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Case Cracked
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Investigator
Against a creature you've Recalled, your Maneuvers can't be critically failed, and your Trip and Shove distances increase by 5 feet.

### Deep Conduit
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Reactive Conduit
Your Conduit discount also covers Battle-trait reactions, and once per round one such reaction is free.

### Pinning Foresight
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Sentinel
When you Foretell a foe and then Counter it, it's off-guard and can't Step until the start of your next turn.

### Precognitive Battlefield
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Sentinel
At the start of combat, allies within 30 feet gain a +1 status bonus to initiative and can't be off-guard during the first round.

---

## 14th-Level Feats

### Foreseen Demise
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Sentinel
When your **Counter** reduces a foe to 0 Hit Points, you regain 1 Attunement and refresh your Counter for the round.

### Greater Conduit
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Deep Conduit
The Force reaction-powers you channel through Premonition gain a +1 circumstance bonus to their roll or DC.

### Phantom Strike
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Shadow
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** After a successful Counter while hidden, you immediately Hide again.

### Quickdraw Corsair
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Corsair
You can draw or holster a weapon and make a ranged Counter as one reaction, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to ranged Counters.

### Untouchable Foresight
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Sentinel
While you have at least 1 Attunement and aren't off-guard, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC.

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Master's Reaction
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You use a Premonition response and a Force-power reaction on the same trigger, paying 1 additional Attunement.

### Relentless Riposte
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Justicar
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** Your Vengeful Premonition also triggers on a normal hit, not only a critical hit.

### Retributive Guard
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Prerequisites** Warden
When you Interpose, you can immediately Counter the attacker (paying 1 Attunement).

### Shared Prescience
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Sentinel
Allies within 30 feet can't be critically hit during the first round of combat.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Ceaseless Watch
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Sentinel
You can use Foretell as a free action once per round.

### Foretell the Field
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Sentinel
Your Foretell affects all foes within 30 feet.

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### Inevitability
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** When you Foretell a foe, the first time it would hit you or a foreseen ally before the start of your next turn, you make it a miss instead.

### Perfect Premonition
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Sentinel
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** One Premonition response you use this round costs no Attunement, whatever it is — even Counter or Foresee fires free — and you can choose which response to use *after* the triggering attack's roll is revealed. (This doesn't raise your three-reaction limit.)
