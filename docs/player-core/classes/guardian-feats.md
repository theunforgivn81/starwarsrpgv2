# Guardian Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Guardian class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower.

> **Sidebar — Lightsaber Form Feats Are Guardian Feats**
> Your deepest pool of options isn't on this page — it's the **Lightsaber Form feats** in **[The Force](the-force.md)**. You can spend any Guardian class feat to learn a new Form or a Form chain feat (Cyclone, Hawk-Bat Swoop, Contentious Riposte, and the rest), and as a Guardian you gain every Form's Guardian Mastery upgrade. Branching into new Forms and deepening the ones you know is the core of building a Guardian; the feats below are everything *else* — the telekinetics, the guarding tools, and the chassis tricks that work no matter which stance you're in.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Challenging Shout  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian, Auditory
An enemy you can see takes a **−1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls that don't include you** until the start of your next turn — the bodyguard's dare.

### Steel Mind
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian
You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against fear and mental effects, and once per round you can reduce the value of one mental condition affecting you by 1 as a free action.

### Telekinetic Leap  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Guardian
**Cost** 1 Attunement
**Effect** You Leap with a burst of the Force, clearing a long gap or closing on a distant foe — the melee guardian's way to reach the firing line.

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Furious Charge  [two-actions]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Guardian
Stride up to your Speed and then make a lightsaber Strike.

### Protective Instinct
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Guardian
Your Intercede range increases to 20 feet, and you can use Intercede against an attack targeting *you* to share its damage reduction with an adjacent ally.

### Telekinetic Shove
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Guardian
Your Force Push gains a Shove rider, and once per turn it costs 1 less Attunement when you use it against a creature in your reach.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Disarming Twist  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Guardian
Make a lightsaber Strike that also attempts to Disarm the target, which defends against your Guardian class DC.

### Sundering Strike
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Guardian
When your lightsaber Strike hits, the target takes a −1 circumstance penalty to AC until the start of your next turn.

### Telekinetic Throw  [two-actions]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Guardian
**Cost** 1 Attunement
**Effect** You hurl an adjacent or grabbed creature; it takes bludgeoning damage and falls prone unless it succeeds at a Reflex save against your Guardian class DC.

---

## 6th-Level Feats

### Cleaving Blow
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Guardian
When a melee Strike reduces a creature to 0 Hit Points, make a lightsaber Strike against an adjacent creature at the same multiple attack penalty.

### Tutaminis
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Guardian
You add **Energy Absorption** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Wall Run
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Guardian
You can Stride along vertical surfaces, falling only if you end your movement on one that can't support you.

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Bodyguard's Reach
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Guardian
Your **Deflect** can protect an adjacent ally from a ranged attack instead of yourself.

### Ground Slam
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Guardian
You add **Force Wave** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Saber Barrier  [two-actions]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Guardian
**Cost** 2 Attunement
**Effect** You project a barrier of deflected energy; you and adjacent allies gain temporary Hit Points equal to your level until the start of your next turn.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Combat Reflexes
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Guardian
You gain an additional reaction each round, which you can use only for Deflect, Intercede, Guardian's Reach, or a reactive lightsaber Strike.

> **Sidebar — The Reaction Ceiling**
> No matter how many reactions your features and feats grant, you can use **at most three per round**. Vigilant Guard, Combat Reflexes, and Greater Combat Reflexes raise you *toward* that ceiling, never past it — so a Guardian chooses which triggers are worth answering.

### Force Speed
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Guardian
You add **Force Celerity** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Shielding Intercede
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Guardian
When you Intercede, the ally also gains a +1 circumstance bonus to AC until the start of your next turn.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Crushing Blow  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Guardian
Make a lightsaber Strike dealing an additional weapon damage die — two additional dice against objects, constructs, or an off-guard target.

### Telekinetic Flight
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Guardian
You add **Telekinetic Flight** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Unyielding  [reaction]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Guardian
**Frequency** once per day
**Trigger** You would be reduced to 0 Hit Points.
**Effect** You stay at 1 Hit Point instead and can keep acting until the end of your turn.

---

## 14th-Level Feats

### Force Bastion
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Guardian
Your Saber Barrier and other defensive auras extend to all allies within 15 feet.

### Greater Combat Reflexes
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Guardian
**Prerequisites** Combat Reflexes
Your extra reactions can also fuel your Form and Force reaction-powers, and once per round you can gain a further reaction (up to the three-reaction limit).

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Master's Deflection
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Guardian
Your **Deflect** works against any ranged attack, not only energy, and when it makes an attack miss you can redirect the shot as a ranged Strike against another creature.

### Relentless Guardian
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Guardian
Your Intercede has no range limit as long as the ally is within your line of sight.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Fluid Forms
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Guardian
Once per turn, you can switch Forms as a free action.

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### Eternal Guardian
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Guardian
**Frequency** once per round
**Trigger** An ally within 30 feet would be reduced to 0 Hit Points.
**Effect** You Intercede to leave them at 1 Hit Point instead, regardless of the source.

### Grand Master
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Guardian
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You gain an extra action you can use only to make a lightsaber Strike or a Form action, and your Guardian Mastery clauses gain a small additional benefit appropriate to each Form.
