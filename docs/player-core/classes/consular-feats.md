# Consular Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain a **Consular class feat**. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a Mandate in their Prerequisites require that Mandate.

> **Sidebar — Powers Beyond Their Limits**
> A handful of feats — Empower Manifest (8th), Quickened Manifest (10th), Overchannel (14th), Twin Manifest (16th), and Cosmic Conduit (18th) — push a power past its normal limits. Each is limited by a Frequency, and some cost Hit Points as well as Attunement.

---

## 1st-Level Feats

### Fleet Center
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You use the Center action as part of another action you take.

### Living Force Sense
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Consular
You have a constant imprecise sense of living creatures within 30 feet.

### Mystic Armor
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Consular
While unarmored, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to AC against ranged attacks.

### Refined Impact
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Consular
Each time you use Telekinetic Impact, you can shape it as an erratic bolt that ignores an amount of the target's resistance equal to your level.

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Deflecting Hand
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Consular
You add **Deflect** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Force Archivist
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Consular
You can use The Force skill to Recall Knowledge about any Force-related topic, and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to such checks.

### Sustaining Will
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You Sustain a Force power as a free action.

### Telekinetic Finesse
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Consular
You can perform delicate manipulation at range with Telekinetic Grasp — picking a lock, disarming a device, keying a console — using the relevant skill at a distance.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Battlefield Medic
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Vitality
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You Manifest Vital Transfer at a range of 30 feet and without paying its Hit Point cost.

### Empowered Impact
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
Your Telekinetic Impact and your Alter forced movement push the target 5 additional feet, and on a critical hit with Telekinetic Impact you knock the target prone.

### Force Levitation  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
You rise and hover, gaining a fly Speed of 10 feet until the start of your next turn.

### Inescapable Grasp
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Telekinesis
A creature grabbed or restrained by your Alter powers takes a –2 circumstance penalty to checks to escape, and takes bludgeoning damage equal to your Level Bonus each time it fails to escape.

### Insidious Suggestion
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular, Mental
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Dominance
Creatures affected by your Mind Trick don't realize they were influenced, even after the effect ends.

### Mind's Shield  [reaction]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular, Mental
**Trigger** You succeed at a Will save against a creature's effect.
**Effect** Psychic feedback lashes back; the creature is off-guard to you until the end of your next turn.

### Overcharge
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Destruction
Once per turn, an Offense power you Manifest gains 1 additional damage die.

### Projected Aura
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
When you Manifest a power, you can project a 15-foot emanation until the start of your next turn. Choose one as you do: allies in the aura gain a +1 status bonus to saves against fear, or enemies in it take a –1 status penalty to attack rolls.

### Tutaminis Mastery
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Serenity
Your Energy Absorption also triggers when you would take force damage, and its Push has no maximum on the temporary Hit Points it grants.

### Untouchable
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Visions
You can never be surprised, and you're never off-guard to creatures you can't see.

## 6th-Level Feats

### Crushing Pressure
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Consular
A creature grabbed or restrained by one of your powers takes damage equal to half your level at the start of each of your turns.

### Force Stride
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Consular
You can move across liquids and up vertical surfaces during your movement, falling only if you end your turn on a surface that can't support you.

### Potent Powers
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Consular
Your healing powers restore 1 additional die, and the persistent damage your powers deal increases by 1.

### Twin Sustain
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Consular
You can Sustain up to two Force powers at once.

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Deep Reserve
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
Your maximum Attunement pool increases by 1.

### Empower Manifest
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You Manifest one power with its damage or healing dice maximized rather than rolled.

### Life Bond
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Vitality
Choose an ally within 30 feet at the start of your turn. Until your next turn, whenever that ally takes damage, you can take half of it in their place.

### Mass Terror
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Dominance
You can Manifest Force Fear as a 15-foot burst, affecting each creature in the area.

### Remote Manifest
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
You can Manifest a power as though you stood in the space of a willing ally or a sensor you can perceive within 100 feet.

### Shared Senses
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
You broadcast your precise senses to allies within 30 feet until the start of your next turn.

### Telekinetic Storm
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Telekinesis
You can Manifest a single-target Alter attack as a 15-foot emanation centered on yourself instead.

### Warding Zone  [two-actions]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Serenity
**Cost** 2 Attunement
**Effect** You create a 15-foot zone for 1 minute; allies within it gain resistance to physical and energy damage equal to your Level Bonus.

---

## 10th-Level Feats

### Absolute Focus
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Consular
Your Sustained powers and your concentration can't be disrupted by taking damage.

### Force Flight
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Consular
You add **Telekinetic Flight** to your repertoire (see *The Force*).

### Quickened Manifest
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You Manifest a single-action power as a free action.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Hurl Foe  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Telekinesis
You hurl a creature you've grabbed or force-moved at another creature within 30 feet. Both take bludgeoning damage equal to 4d6, and the struck creature must succeed at a Reflex save against your Consular class DC or fall prone.

### Lasting Domination
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Dominance
Your Dominate Mind lasts up to 10 minutes when it takes hold, and the target takes a –2 circumstance penalty to its saves to end the effect.

### Perfect Clarity
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular, Fortune
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Visions
You automatically disbelieve illusions you observe, and once per round you can reroll a check by glimpsing the moment ahead.

### Phase Step  [one-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You step through the Force, teleporting up to 30 feet to a space you can see.

### Rapid Regeneration
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Vitality
When you restore Hit Points to an ally, you can instead grant them fast healing 5 for 1 minute.

### Restorative Aura  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular, Healing
**Cost** 2 Attunement
**Effect** Each ally in a 15-foot emanation regains Hit Points equal to 2d8 plus your Level Bonus.

### Wide Aperture
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Consular
The areas of your area powers increase by one size step.

---

## 14th-Level Feats

### Annihilating Bolt
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Mandate of Destruction
Your Offense powers ignore an amount of the target's resistance equal to your level.

### Deny Death  [reaction]
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per day per ally
**Trigger** An ally within 30 feet would be reduced to 0 Hit Points or die.
**Effect** The ally remains at 1 Hit Point instead.

### Overchannel
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You Manifest a power you can't currently afford, paying the missing Attunement as an equal number of Hit Points. You're then fatigued.

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Greater Empower
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Consular
**Prerequisites** Empower Manifest
Empower Manifest's Frequency becomes once per round.

### Twin Manifest
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You Manifest two single-action powers as a single two-action activity.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Cosmic Conduit
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Consular
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** You channel a power at its utmost. The next power you Manifest this turn ignores the Incapacitation trait, and its damage or healing dice increase by one.

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### One with the Force
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Consular
The Force flows through you almost without cost: reduce the Attunement cost of every power you Manifest by 1 (minimum 0).

### Selfless Sacrifice  [two-actions]
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Consular, Healing
You expend your entire remaining Attunement pool. An ally you touch regains Hit Points equal to the Attunement spent times your level, or is restored to life if they died this combat. You then become drained 2.
