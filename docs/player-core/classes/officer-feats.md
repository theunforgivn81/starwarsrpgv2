# Officer Feats

At 1st level and every even level you gain an **Officer class feat** — and at 1st level, a **bonus class feat** you must spend on a feat with the **[Directive]** trait. The feats below are listed by the level at which they become available; you can always take a feat of your level or lower. Feats that name a leadership style in their Prerequisites require it.

Many feats here **grant a new Directive** — they're how your command menu grows. Knowing more Directives never lets you issue more than one per round; it gives you more to choose from.

> **Sidebar — Orders, Reactions, and the Menu**
> A **[Directive]** feat adds an order to your repertoire; you still issue only **one [Directive] per round**. Feats marked **[reaction]** are *untagged* commands — warnings and rescues that fire in the same round as your issued Directive, limited only by the **three-reactions-per-round** ceiling everyone shares. Same-type status bonuses don't stack, so two Officers giving the same order don't double it.

## 1st-Level Feats

### Cutting Taunt  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Officer, [Flourish], Emotion, Mental
Attempt to Demoralize a foe using your Officer class DC, rattling it with a precise barb.

### Get Moving!  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Each ally who can sense you may Stride toward a point you indicate.

### On My Mark!  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Choose an enemy. The next ally to attack it this round gains a **+1 status bonus** to that attack roll and its damage.

### Steady!  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Until the start of your next turn, each ally who can sense you gains a **+1 status bonus to AC and saving throws**.

### Take Cover!  [one-action]
**Feat 1** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Each ally who can sense you may Take Cover or Step to cover.

## 2nd-Level Feats

### Heads Up!  [reaction]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Officer, Auditory, Visual
**Trigger** An ally you can see is targeted by an attack.
**Effect** The ally gains a **+2 circumstance bonus to AC** against that attack. (This is an untagged command — it coexists with the Directive you issued this round.)

### Pep Talk!  [one-action]
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
One ally who can sense you gains **temporary Hit Points equal to your Charisma modifier** and a **+1 status bonus to their next attack or skill check** this round.

### Quick Orders
**Feat 2** · **Traits** Officer
Once per round, you can issue a Directive and Step as part of the same activity.

---

## 4th-Level Feats

### Improvised Mastery
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Frequency** once per hour
**Effect** You can attempt a skill check that requires you to be trained even while untrained, using your level as your proficiency.

### Look Alive!  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Each ally who can sense you may Seek, gaining a **+1 status bonus** to find hidden creatures or objects.

### Shake It Off!  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
One ally who can sense you reduces the value of one condition (such as frightened or clumsy) by 1.

### Silent Signal
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** From the Shadows
You can issue any Directive with hand signals alone, with no auditory or visual tell that reveals you as its source.

### Vanguard's Order
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** From the Front
An ally who repositions on one of your movement Directives also gains a **+1 status bonus to damage** on its next Strike this round.

### Dazzling Display  [one-action]
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer, [Flourish]
**Prerequisites** In the Spotlight
Attempt a Performance check against the Will DCs of foes in a 15-foot emanation; those who fail are **off-guard** until the start of your next turn.

### Tactical Network
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** HoloNet Director
Allies who can sense you ignore the cover of any foe you've assessed with Digital Assessment! this combat.

### Suppressing Orders
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** Guns Blazing
When you designate a foe with a firepower Directive, allies' ranged Strikes against it also reduce its Speed by 5 feet on a hit (to a minimum of half) until the start of your next turn.

### Field Triage
**Feat 4** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** Through Desperate Times
Your rally Directives (such as Rally! and Steady On!) also restore Hit Points equal to your Charisma modifier to each affected ally.

## 6th-Level Feats

### Focus Fire!  [one-action]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Designate a foe; allies who can sense you gain a **+1 status bonus to attacks** against it until the start of your next turn. When it drops, you may re-designate a new foe as a free action.

### Press the Advantage!  [one-action]
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
The first time each directed ally **hits** your designated foe this round, it adds a **status bonus to that Strike's damage** equal to your Charisma modifier.

### Ready Directive
**Feat 6** · **Traits** Officer
You can hold a Directive, choosing a trigger; it's issued when the trigger occurs rather than on your turn (this still counts as your Directive for the round).

---

## 8th-Level Feats

### Encore
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** In the Spotlight
Once per round, you can repeat the temporary-Hit-Point or morale effect of your last morale Directive on one ally who can sense you, as a free action.

### Follow-Up
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** When a directed ally hits the foe you designated this round, you can make a Strike against that foe as a reaction.

### Hang On!  [reaction]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer, Auditory, Visual
**Trigger** An ally within 30 feet is reduced to 0 Hit Points or starts to die.
**Effect** The ally can attempt a flat check to stay at 1 Hit Point, and you restore Hit Points to it equal to your level. (Untagged — it coexists with your Directive.)

### Killing Coordination
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** From the Shadows
A directed ally's first Strike against an off-guard foe each round deals one additional weapon damage die.

### Mockery!  [one-action]
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Emotion, Mental
One enemy within 60 feet attempts a Will save against your Officer class DC; on a failure it's **off-guard** until the start of your next turn.

### Overwhelming Volley
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** Guns Blazing
The contingent free Strike from Ready Arms! can come from **whichever** of two allies you choose — but it's still only **one** Strike.

### Predictive Analysis
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** HoloNet Director
Once per round, an ally who can sense you can reroll an attack roll against a foe you've assessed with Digital Assessment!.

### Reissue
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Frequency** once per round
**Effect** You can repeat the Directive you issued last round as a free action, sustaining the same order.

### Unbreakable Formation
**Feat 8** · **Traits** Officer
**Prerequisites** From the Front
Allies adjacent to you can't be moved against their will or knocked prone.

## 10th-Level Feats

### Expanded Command
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Officer
Your Directives reach **every** ally who can sense you, with no range limit within sight or earshot.

### Fall Back!  [one-action]
**Feat 10** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Each ally who can sense you may Stride; this movement doesn't trigger reactions.

---

## 12th-Level Feats

### Battle Plan!  [two-actions]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Officer, [Directive], Auditory, Visual
Choose two of the following and assign each to a different ally who can sense you, until the start of your next turn: a +1 status bonus to attacks, a +1 status bonus to AC, a Step, or a Seek.

### Double Time  [free-action]
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Officer
**Frequency** once per minute
**Trigger** You issue a Directive.
**Effect** Issue a **second** Directive this round (it can't use a Lead by Example mode).

### Veteran's Instinct
**Feat 12** · **Traits** Officer
You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to initiative, and you can't be made off-guard at the start of an encounter.

---

## 14th-Level Feats

### Tactical Reaction  [reaction]
**Feat 14** · **Traits** Officer, Auditory, Visual
**Trigger** An ally who can sense you is about to act and would benefit from a Directive.
**Effect** Issue a one-action Directive. (This is your Directive for the round if you haven't issued one.)

---

## 16th-Level Feats

### Grand Strategy!
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Officer
When you issue a Directive that offers multiple optional effects, it applies **two** of them at once.

### Greater Directives
**Feat 16** · **Traits** Officer
The status bonuses your Directives grant increase by 1 (for example, Get-the-Job-Done grants +2), to a maximum matching your highest proficiency tier.

---

## 18th-Level Feats

### Legendary Commander
**Feat 18** · **Traits** Officer
You can issue Directives even when you'd normally be unable to be heard or seen by some means (such as in vacuum or silence), reaching allies by any sense they share with you, and your Lead by Example modes deepen their effects.

---

## 20th-Level Feats

### One Unit
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Officer
**Frequency** once per minute
**Effect** Each ally who can sense you may immediately take a free **Step** and gains a status surge — a +2 status bonus to attacks and saves until the start of your next turn.

### Perfect Coordination
**Feat 20** · **Traits** Officer
You can issue **two** Directives each round (each a full Directive, including Lead by Example). They grant bonuses, not extra actions.
